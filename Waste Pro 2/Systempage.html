waste management

The waste management project is a holistic initiative focused on sustainable waste handling. It includes the establishment of sorting and recycling centers, public awareness campaigns, and technology-driven optimization of waste collection routes. Emphasizing a circular economy and community engagement, the project aims to reduce environmental impact, promote responsible waste disposal, and contribute to long-term ecological sustainability.
